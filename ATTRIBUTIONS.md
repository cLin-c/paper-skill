# Attributions

## Research-Paper-Writing-Skills

The section-writing architecture in paper-skill v0.7 was informed by ideas in:

- Master-cai, `Research-Paper-Writing-Skills`: https://github.com/Master-cai/Research-Paper-Writing-Skills
- The upstream notes credited by that project to Prof. Peng Sida: https://github.com/pengsida/learning_research

Concepts adapted and independently rewritten include section-specific progressive disclosure, reverse outlining, reviewer-first clarity checks, and motivation/design/advantage-style method explanation. paper-skill expresses these concepts through its own evidence-traceable contracts, Need–Design–Effect framework, cross-file verification, and original synthetic examples.
